About

×

This tool was inspired by the Interactive Sagittal Section by Daniel Currie Hall. The division of linguistic features adheres to features in hul’q’umi’num’ and hən̓q̓əmin̓əm̓, as laid out on xwulmuhw.

The mid-sagittal image is a partial replication of that used by Currie Hall [The general structure of this sagittal section is based on diagrams in The sounds of language: An introduction to phonetics, by Henry Rogers].
Illustrations for voicing, glottalization, and some manners were designed by Sonya Bird and Chloë Farr.
Orthography follows that used by the hul’q’umi’num’ Language and Culture Society.
